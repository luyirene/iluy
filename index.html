A little bit about me

Growing up, I’ve always had the strong desire to work in the creative field. I spent countless hours in art classes painting landscapes and sketching still lifes. After school I would dabble with designing personal websites and taught myself basic HTML/CSS. Somewhere down the line I found myself pursuing a doctorate in the healthcare field instead. It was an avenue where I could express compassion for others and help improve lives. While I found great value in the work I was doing, the creator in me wanted more.

When I was introduced to UX design, I did some research and spoke with a few established UX designers to gain more insight into the field. As a ubiquitous and highly relevant practice, the variety of opportunities in user experience excited me. UX design provides a platform where I can unite my desires to create and impact lives, to design user-centered solutions.
